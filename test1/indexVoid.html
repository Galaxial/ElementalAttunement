Elemental Attunement: Void

The Void element generates a kind black energy with extreme destructive potential. This black energy pulls things towards it, but can only be created in open air. The Void-element is also quite affected by IWC, so even if the Voidelement has considerable range, they will not be able to create this energy near another person who doesn’t want it to be there – and, given what it does, nobody will want it to be there. A Void-element has an average range of 15- 20m, and can create larger amounts of this energy the closer they are to it. The Void element is also extremely taxing to use and notoriously finicky – losing focus can disrupt it, moving can disrupt it, using auxiliary powers will always disrupt it – and on top of that, it requires a lot of energy. This means that many Void-elements have trouble maintaining HEA equilibrium, which is doubly problematic because the Voidelement will cause negative side effects pretty much immediately for anybody who attempts a non-elemental energy transfer to keep it going. Void-elements are typically withdrawn and cold, though they are not necessarily amoral – it’s just that most of them are plagued by a feeling of apartness that they can never quite pinpoint the source of. They are rarely people of happy mediums, and oftentimes prioritize extreme courses of action simply because they are extreme. At their best, they can be decisive, introspective people willing to take on very difficult jobs. At worst, they can be dispassionate, humourless, and self-serious. They’re almost never imposing people, and hate asking for favours. There isn’t enough data on them to make a generalization about the kinds of auxiliary powers that they manifest. They make up .5% of elementalists.
