Elemental Attunement: Air

The Air element controls gasses around median temperature. The minimal amount of energy required to manipulate these gasses is at density of about 1.10kg/m3, and extends about .5kg/m3 in both directions – though as usual, skilled elementalists can control much denser or less dense gasses. Air-elements can exert control over matter that is significantly farther away compared to other coarse manipulation elementalists – they have an average range of 70m and some particularly powerful Air-elements retaining some measure of control up to 300m. Air-elements can accelerate and decelerate gasses to create strong gusts of wind, sometimes fast enough to do serious damage. They can, with enough effort, control all manner of gasses – some Airelements have been able to control gasses as dense as chlorine (though no elementalist has demonstrated the coarse ability needed to control tungsten hexafluoride). They can also create small vacuums by increasing and decreasing air. They cannot violate another human’s IWC to any meaningful extent, which means that suffocating somebody by controlling the air inside their lungs is not possible unless the target actively wants it – and the human breathing reflex will force them to stop wanting it pretty fast, no matter what. Air-elements are typically outgoing and carefree, not prone to extensive sentimentality and with no patience for self-pity. This means they can be fun, gregarious, and adaptive, but can also be prone to fickleness, rash decisionmaking, and discomfort with negative emotions – in both themselves and others.
