Elemental Attunement: Magnetism

The Magnetism element conditionally creates electromagnetic fields. They can be used to magnetically attract or repel things, or to create electromagnetic radiation. The frequency of this radiation is dependant on the elementalist, but lower frequencies tend to be easier to produce. Most Magnetism-elements can create electromagnetic radiation up to 250MHz, though some have been able to create radiation as high-frequency as microwaves. While they theoretically have the capacity to detect and Extremely Low Frequency waves, they do not have the ability to intuitively process them – this ability is restricted to waves above 30MHz. Magnetism-elements’ powers manifest visibly as weaker/less flexible versions of Metal-elements’ powers – they can move magnetic materials around, but only towards them or away from them. The real utility of their power comes in being able to detect low-frequency electromagnetic radiation. They can produce signals that can be interpreted by electronic devices and converted into 44 sound, allowing them to create “phantom voices” that exist only on the airwaves and were never spoken by a human. They can block or intercept most wireless communication. They can figure out roughly where a signal is coming from and track it down over great distances. This is all facilitated by a massive amount of intuitive control – Magnetism-elements’ ability to recognize their element is perhaps orders of magnitude stronger than any other element. Magnetismelements can’t simultaneously process more information than, say, a Soundelement, but they can find information faster, oftentimes being able to pick out a single signal to listen to from hundreds of others. Their zone of immediate control is comparatively small – they can easily influence signals within 20m, but most have difficulty beyond that. As with all Transformative Manipulation Elements, they can only emit electromagnetic radiation from their hands. Their zone of intuitive control, however, can be upwards of 5km. Magnetism-elements are often quiet but easygoing – though they can become skittish if they spend too much time listening for signals, this doesn’t seem to be something they’re naturally predisposed to, but rather something that happens to them. They tend to cope with drastic changes well, and while they don’t actively try to suppress their emotions, they aren’t prone to give them too much thought, either. At their best they are friendly, undemanding and levelheaded people, but they can easily slip into apathetic, boring laziness if they don’t stay focused on the people around them. Like Gravity-elements, they don’t often manifest particularly dynamic or demanding auxiliary powers. They are rare, accounting for 3% of total elementalists.
