Elemental Attunement: Earth

Earth controls naturally solid non-conductive minerals – if a mineral could conduct electricity at absolute zero, then the Earth element cannot control it. The particular conditions of the minerals controlled must rest well below the object’s melting point but well above absolute zero. In this sense, it can be viewed as part of a spectrum with the Ice and Lava elements, which control very hot substances and very cold substances respectively. Talented Earth-elements can fling boulders or smash them into sand, feel pressure exerted against any rock in the area (for example, footsteps), and talented Earth-elements can typically control up to 1500 kg of at once, with the amount decreasing as the Earth gets further from the elementalist, with most Earth-elements losing control at around 12m. The Earth element generally can be controlled by elementalists if it is within 80° C of their body temperature, though strong elementalists can control it beyond that range. The Earth element also gets more difficult to control the less dense it is. Median weight is around 600kg, which means some Earth-elements will find it difficult to control very light objects. The Earth element tends to manifest in people with committed, forthright personalities. Earth elements are often quite shameless – this can manifest as strong ethical convictions that resist peer pressure well, monomaniacal pursuit of a particular interest with no regard for how socially acceptable it is, and sometimes as plain stubbornness. It is commonly associated with strength auxiliary powers, which occur in Earth-elements at a high rate. It is 26 one of the most common elements, making up about 8% of the total elementalist population.
