Elemental Attunement: Sound

The Sound element creates, stops, or alters very small vibrations. While these waves do not strictly have to be within audible frequencies, most Soundelements can only exert power over waves in a frequency range audible to the human ear. Unlike Coarse Elements, where the median temperature is fixed to the body temperature of the elementalist, the median frequency of Sound is fixed for each Sound-element under all conditions, but varies from individual to individual. Typically, it’s found somewhere in the 10kHz range, though it is sometimes as low as 1kHz or as high as 20kHz. The further a sound gets from this median frequency, the more energy it will take to produce/control, with most Sound-elements losing control of frequencies more than 10kHz away from this – given what the median sound is, this generally means that Sound-elements have a much easier time creating low sounds than high ones. They do not have a median amplitude, with a louder signal requiring a proportionally larger effort. Most Sound-elements can create noises as loud as 20dB at close range, and can create voice-level noises quite far away from them – sometimes up to 200m. Like all other elementalists, Soundelements can their element at ranges they can’t actively manipulate, which means it’s completely possible that a Sound-element could eavesdrop on a conversation happening half a kilometre away. While this is unlikely (Sound-elements have a harder time listening at range when there are other noises around them), with enough practice they can process a huge amount of auditory data simultaneously and sift out even the quietest of sounds. Sound-elements can create noises loud enough to temporarily deafen somebody and cause permanent long-term damage. They can silence noises or simply reduce them, which means that Sound-elements can’t really deafen other Sound-elements without exerting a drastically superior amount of energy. They can detect the faintest of sounds, and some can use this to create a personal kind 42 of sonar. Some Sound-elements with a particular gift for creating strong signals can create noises that are loud and low enough to shake or even damage solid objects. They can intuitively identify frequencies, which means that they can both tune guitars perfectly without a tuner, and get really, really annoyed by instruments that are even slightly out of tune. Sound-elements are often quiet, and not just out of necessity – while they need quiet to use their powers effectively, they also tend to get overwhelmed by groups of people, and especially by groups of people they don’t know. They have a tendency to be patient and attentive people who love to help others, but they can also become extremely suspicious of people who don’t accept – or sometimes even ask for – their help. They are, at their best, kind and reasonable people, and at their worst, they can be paranoid or needy. Sound-elements tend to manifest auxiliary powers that create or influence other objects – shieling is common, as is repairing or healing. They are not entirely uncommon, accounting for 5% of elementalists.
