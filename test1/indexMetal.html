Elemental Attunement: Metal

Metal controls naturally solid conductive minerals under about the same conditions that Earth can control nonconductive minerals – too hot or too cold and the Elementalist will have to exert increasingly demanding levels of energy to maintain control. As with Earth, the median temperature for control is body temperature, and controlling a non-solid metal at median temperature requires exerting a level of energy equivalent to the level of energy that would be needed to manipulate that metal were it frozen. A typical Metal-element can exert control over metals within 50° C of median temperature, though some have a greater range. This means the average Metal-Element will have a very difficult time controlling roomtemperature mercury, whose freezing point of -38° C puts it 75° C away from median temperature, but will have no trouble controlling liquid gallium, which melts at 29° C. Metal-elements can levitate, throw, bend, and break metal. Talented Metal-elements can change its brittleness and reshape it without having to bend or break it. Metal-elements typically can control between 100 and 400 kg of metal in optimal conditions, with the amount decreasing at range, in temperatures far from the median, and in non-solid states. Metal-elements typically lose the ability to manipulate their element at a distance of 15m. The Metal element tends to manifest in people that are pragmatic and reliable – though, perhaps surprisingly, not inflexible. At their best, Metal-Elements can be efficient and fearless friends. At worst, they can come to see themselves as above the law, or just be quite self-centred. Metal-elements tend to orient themselves towards their friends, but don’t have a difficult time respecting boundaries – ask and they will do it, but don’t expect them to force help upon you. Metal is associated with a variety of auxiliary powers, and is one of the most common elements, accounting for about 7% of elementalists.
