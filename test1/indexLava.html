Elemental Attunement: Lava

The Lava element is the inverse of the Ice element. Ice cools things down, Lava heats them up. Ice can only control solids, Lava can only control liquids. Ice is predisposed to controlling things that are liquid near or below the median temperature, while Lava is predisposed to controlling things that are solid near or above the median temperature. Consequently, Lavaelements in combat rely primarily on melting down whatever they can get their hands on, and then throwing that melted rock/metal/whatever at whoever they want out of their way. Lava-elements have a dual focus much like Ice-elements do, except they focus on transforming things into hotter things and manipulating those hot things. They have a range and a maximum weight that are similar to, if not identical to, Ice’s range/max weight. Lava does have some idiosyncrasies, though – combustion of any kind interferes with their ability to control things, which means they best direct their powers at things that will melt before they burst into flames. Fortunately, unlike Ice, Lava-elements have no problems using their powers on things like stone and metal, which they can melt down very quickly – often in mere seconds. Lava-elements are typically stoic, reserved people, capable of preserving under extremely unpleasant circumstances. At their best, they are determined, loyal, and giving – they don’t often place all that much value on their own possessions, and maintaining their own integrity is of paramount importance to them. If they aren’t careful, they can develop a bit of a martyr complex, or may 33 come across as moody, standoffish, or just boring. Lava elements often develop close-range sensory powers, and are rarer than Ice-elements, accounting for about 4% of all elementalists.
