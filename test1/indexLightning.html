Elemental Attunement: Lightning

The Lightning element is one of several elements that deal with electromagnetic forces, and is by far the most common of them. Lightning-elements can generate an electric field, and direct electric currents. The exact limitations of the Lightning element are more complicated, however. Lightning-elements can generate electromagnetic fields of varying strength, with most being able to create fields that well exceed the breakdown voltage of air. They can also use their power to create electric arcs, though IWC prevents them from doing this inside the body of another person. This doesn’t mean that they can’t shock people, though – just that they need to exert more energy to push an electrical current “off-course” and send it through somebody. Lightning-elements can typically create around 300kV of electricity, which is enough to bridge a 10m gap, though many can generate more if they aren’t focused on sending it somewhere. Lightning-elements can create brief electrostatic discharges, or maintain sustained currents inside them. They can conduct currents that they have not produced through themselves without harm – though no Lightning-element can do this with an infinitely strong current. Most Lightning-elements can redirect a number of amperes quite a bit higher than the number that they could produce themselves – though if they are currently using their powers to channel a current through themselves, increasing the amperes of the current can cause them to quickly exceed their limits and suffer an electric shock like a normal person. IWC reduces the effectiveness of Lightning as a weapon, though every Lightningelement can produce enough electricity to stun at range, and some can do serious damage or kill. Lightning-elements can sustain an electric arc for a very brief time (typically less than a second) but can sustain a current within themselves for significantly longer. If the current is low enough, they can do this nigh-indefinitely, or while simultaneously creating electric arcs. Lightning-elements are typically outgoing and agreeable, sometimes to the point of being irritating. They typically fear boredom above all else, and can, 34 if not careful, tend towards recklessness or hedonism. Lightning-elements can manifest a variety of powers, but tend towards those that can be used on other people willingly. They are quite common, making up about 7% of all elementalists
