Elemental Attunement: Light

The Light element consists of two major components: light emission and light manipulation. A Light-element can produce light anywhere within range (which is typically around 15m) and they can control exactly what this light looks like. All Light-elements have a median frequency around green, and it takes an equal amount of energy to increase or decrease frequencies from here. Because the difference in hertz between green and ultraviolet is smaller than the difference between green and infrared, Light-elements typically have an easier time producing purples and blues than they do reds and oranges – though almost all Light-elements can produce any visible wavelength with ease. They can also produce ultraviolet light, though typically stopping before the near-ultraviolet range. Infrared light can be produced with difficulty. Some Light-elementalists with an unusually high median frequency cannot produce visible red light, though this is typically compensated by an increased capacity for ultraviolet light production. They can produce lights of up to 8000m. They can also redirect light or alter its frequency. These abilities combine to give a Light-element a high degree of control over what somebody sees. They can create incredibly realistic illusions, blindingly bright flashes, or coherent beams. If a Light-element is skilled enough they can increase their light’s coherence to the point where it forms a laser. Elementalists focused on more subtle use of Light often learn to bend light around them, rendering them invisible at a distance. Light-elements can also learn how to direct light from a wide area into their eyes without compromising its organization, allowing them to see at greater distances or in darker conditions. Light-elements have a natural intuitive control of well-lit areas, but the range of their intuitive 46 control is both quite small (20m, barely larger than their manipulative range) and also limited by line of sight. Light-elements tend to be outgoing, talkative, and confident. They need groups of people that they can trust, and fear dishonesty – perhaps because they aren’t often good at detecting it. They aren’t inclined to lead, but are nonetheless encouraging and upbeat, and often excel in both moral and practical support. However, they can also be blunt and selfish, and they can have a hard time empathizing with people. Like Shadow-Elements, they commonly manifest the Photonic Solid Lattice auxiliary power, which allows Light-elements to convert areas of extreme, coherent brightness into something that resembles a solid object. When they don’t manifest that auxiliary power, they frequently manifest healing or amplification powers. They are not uncommon, making up about 6% of all elementalists.
