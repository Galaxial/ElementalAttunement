Elemental Attunement: Dust

Dust is the most common of the Quasi-Elements, though it is still rarer than any non-Quasi-Element. The DustElement destroys matter within a rigidly defined area, breaking it down into miniscule pieces that often look like grains of sand. A Dust-element will focus on an area that they wish to disintegrate. A translucent, cuboid lattice of light will appear around that area. When the lattice is fully formed, it will briefly turn into an opaque cuboid, and then fade, leaving a fine powder behind. Dust-elements can form this lattice faster the smaller it is, though most find making miniscule lattices also quite difficult. They can increase the speed/size of this lattice by creating it closer to themselves – though this is of limited utility, because they typically have a very small range to begin with – rarely over 5m. Dust is noticeable for being totally unaffected by IWC. Dust-elements are typically dreamy, romantic people, who act on their feelings in the long-term. They aren’t necessarily impulsive, but they don’t put much effort into understanding why they want the things that they want. They can be creative and friendly if they keep themselves a bit grounded, and awkward and insensitive if they don’t. There aren’t enough Dust-elements to determine what kind of auxiliary power they’re inclined to manifest – they consist of around .7% of elementalists.
