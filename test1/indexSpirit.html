Elemental Attunement: Spirit

The Spirit element is the rarest element and is not well understood. It seems to be able to control things across the planar boundary, because it reacts strongly to HEA nodes, and especially strongly to people with elemental powers. A Spirit-Element has an intuitive ability to identify people with powers, and the more powerful the person, the greater the distance at which they can be sensed. The Spirit element can cause shadowy figures to manifest. They can “direct” these figures, though not in the sense that an Earth-element could direct a rock – it’s more like “suggesting.” They can interact with living things, and can interact with people that have elemental powers much more easily. A Spirit element can use them to obstruct HEA nodes, which can inhibit an elementalists powers. The Spirit-element’s indirect control of these figures functions as a kind of IWC workaround, as they can act on others’ bodies without difficulty. It’s possible that Spirit is less a Quasi-Element and more a kind of “transplanar biotic element” – Blood controls the body, Psionics controls the mind, and Spirit controls the HEA nodes. This would explain why the Spirit element works best on other elementalists, and provides a possible explanation for the shadowy figures: they are inhabitants of the Elemental Plane that are being controlled from the Terrestrial Plane. Spirit-elements tend to be more social than the other Quasi-elements, but other than this, they don’t have very many easily definable trends in personality characteristics. They all report a feeling of distance from their own element – they don’t feel like they control it, they just suggest what it should do. There isn’t much data to extrapolate trends in auxiliary powers from. They make up just .3% of elementalists.
