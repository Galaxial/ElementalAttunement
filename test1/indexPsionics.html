Elemental Attunement: Psionics

The Psionic element deals with the manipulation of neural activity, which means that more than any other Biotic element, Psionics runs up against IWC. Since the object of Psionic manipulation exists entirely within the body, and there are few reasons a person would willingly permit a somebody to rummage through their minds, the average Psionicselement has to be content with much less specific information. They can detect general states of minds, encourage or discourage certain emotions, or completely mind-control things that have brains but no pronounced IWC – so, most nonhuman animals. A Psionics-element typically has a practical range of around 30m, but can detect brain activity (but not specific thoughts) at distances approaching 500m. They do not need to see a person to do this, which means it’s very hard to sneak up on them. The list of Psionic abilities that a Psionics-element can be reasonably expected to use is still fairly long – they make good lie detectors, they can calm down panicking people, communicate with one-way telepathy provided the subject is willing, and encourage or discourage certain courses of action (though they have to be careful, as most people are at least somewhat aware of when they are using IWC. This means that if the Psionics-element exerts too much effort, the subject will become aware that somebody is attempting to influence their thoughts). Skilled Psionics-elements can cause or cure intense headaches, induce sleepiness, expedite forgetfulness, and, if the target dazed enough to inhibit their IWC, do something that approaches mind control. Psionics-elements lose the ability to control brains within seconds of death, but can quickly search the mind of a corpse if they want. Given the time limit, it’s unlikely that they’ll find much of use. 38 Psionics-elements are difficult to characterize, as being constantly aware of peoples’ emotional states would have a pretty strong impact on anybody – it’s quite unclear if the personality traits associated with them are innate or simply learned through the kinds of experiences that the Psionics element is conducive of. They always want to understand people around them – this makes them kind and attentive people if they have a high opinion of humans, and tremendously manipulative and snobbish if they don’t. They tend to manifest auxiliary powers that are very physical, like creating shields, speed, or teleportation – this is presumably because auxiliary powers that are subtle and mind-oriented are already sourced from the Psionics element, precluding their use. They are uncommon but not altogether rare, making up about 4% of the world’s elementalists.
